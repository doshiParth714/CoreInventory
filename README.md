# CoreInventory
CoreInventory is a modular Inventory Management System (IMS) built to replace manual registers, Excel sheets, and scattered tracking methods with a centralized, real-time platform. Designed for inventory managers and warehouse staff, it streamlines every stock-related operation from goods receipt to delivery and internal transfers.
Key Features:

📦 Product Management — Create and manage products with SKU, category, unit of measure, and stock levels.
🚚 Receipts & Deliveries — Handle incoming vendor stock and outgoing customer shipments with automatic stock updates.
🔄 Internal Transfers — Move stock across warehouses, floors, or racks with full ledger logging.
🛠️ Stock Adjustments — Reconcile physical counts with recorded inventory, with every change auto-logged.
📊 Live Dashboard — Real-time KPIs for total stock, low/out-of-stock alerts, pending receipts, deliveries, and transfers.
🏭 Multi-Warehouse Support — Manage and filter operations across multiple warehouse locations.
🔔 Low Stock Alerts — Get notified before you run out of critical items.
🔐 Secure Auth — OTP-based login and password reset.
